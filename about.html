About Hotel Delicosa

When two days work wonders... Experience deep relaxation and regeneration at our 7132 Thermal Baths and at our spa. From detox and anti-stress treatments to signature ESPA treatments, it is a sense of pure well-being from head to toe. Our spa is a perfect treat for girlfriends, mothers-to-be and even men. Enjoy moments of happiness “à deux”.
